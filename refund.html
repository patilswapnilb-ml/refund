Refunds Related

What are the modes of refund available after cancellation?

The different modes of refund available are:

  • Payment mode: Cash on Delivery
    • UPI (Amount less than Rs 1000): Refund will be processed to the UPI account for which UPI ID has to be shared.
    • Gift Card (Amount less than Rs 10000): Refund will be processed to the Gift Card account.
    • Bank Account (IMPS/NEFT): Refund will be processed to the bank account for which bank details have to be shared.
  • Payment mode: Prepaid
    • Back to Source: Refund will be processed to the payment mode (Debit/Credit card, NEFT, UPI) used to pay for the order.
    • Gift Card (Amount less than Rs 10000): Refund will be processed to the Gift Card account.

Why does it take time for the refund amount to be credited when it was already processed by Click&Cart?

Once the refund is processed, the bank takes some time to reverse your initial transaction and complete the refund. The refund amount will be visible in your account within the timeframe provided to you. If the refund amount is not credited after the specified timeframe, please contact your bank with the refund reference/UTR number.

When will the refund amount for my order(s) be processed?

Refund timelines depend on the refund reason:

  • Cancellation (before shipment): Immediate.
  • Cancellation (after shipment): After the courier confirms the shipment return.
  • Returns (with seller pick-up): After the product is picked up and reaches the seller.
  • Returns (self-shipment): After the item is delivered to the seller.

What are the refund timelines if I cancel or return a product?

Refund timelines based on payment mode:

  • Debit Card: 7–9 business days.
  • Credit Card: 7–9 business days.
  • Net Banking: 3–7 business days.
  • Cash on Delivery (IMPS): 1 business day.
  • EMI (Standard/No Cost): 7–9 business days.
  • Click&Cart Pay Later: 24–48 hours.
  • Gift Card: 24 hours.
  • PhonePe Wallet: 24 hours.
  • PhonePe UPI: 1 business day.
  • PhonePe Credit/Debit Card: 7 business days.

Note: Refunds are credited to the original payment source. For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds are processed via IMPS.
